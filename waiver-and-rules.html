5 Star Exotic Cars Club (5SEC) Rules and Etiquette

Absolutely no using and/or disbursing drugs and/or alcohol while at 5SEC events (car shows, public meets, cruises, etc.) except where alcohol is permitted at social gatherings.

Absolutely no racing, excessive revving of engines or burn-outs on public roads, during club events or meets. Racing is to be done on the racetrack.

Absolutely no external car show postings on 5SEC social media without prior permission. Please reach out to a 5SEC Board of Director member before posting any car shows or events onto any group page relating to 5SEC.

At all times, members are expected to show respect to other 5SEC members, other car clubs as well as the communities we serve. Discrimination against members or non-members for any reason (sex, race, gender, religion, political preference, disability, etc.) will not be tolerated. Please report any such misbehavior to a 5SEC Board of Director member immediately.

As with any event, we expect each member to keep a watchful eye on other 5SEC member cars and/or property . Please report any suspicious activity to a 5SEC Board of Directors member immediately.

Members are requested to assist in club sponsored events where and when possible. The goal is to come together as one focused group to make 5SEC events as meaningful and fun as possible.

Member cars must be clean and well maintained. Postponing any maintenance can be dangerous to fellow members and the general public.

If a member does not adhere to the 5SEC rules and their actions or activities harm the club or its members, that member may be excused from 5SEC.

As the club evolves, these rules and regulations are subject to change.
